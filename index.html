CHI-KA is a Japanese singer. Born and raised in Okinawa. Because CHI-KA has always been surrounded by a beautiful nature and good music, she now has very beautiful and unforgettable voice. In 2009, she collaborated with Ryota Nozaki (Jazztronik) to sing "Ponyo on the Cliff by the Sea" from the cover album "Ghibli meets Bossa Nova." Her debut album, "With You" was released on April 7th, 2010. She sings the opening song of "Rune Factory Oceans," or "Tides of Destiny" as it's known internationally (PS3/Wii role-playing game developed by Neverland Co.), which was released on February 24th, 2011. On February 26th, 2011, her album "With You Rune Factory Oceans Deluxe Edition" was released. From May to October, 2013, her song 「Wings of Heart」 was featured in a TV commercial for TOYOTA Okinawa.
